About the author:

The Author is a 14-year-old student of Philippine Science High School-Central Luzon Campus. He is a chubby and vibrant teenager who loves to play Cosmoteer and among us. He is very much anxious about going back to the old normal like face to face classes and hopes that a vaccine will be discovered soon. He found inspiration for the satirical essay on the pandemic and thought of using jobless people to help find a cure. “The Facility”, in reality, is a laboratory where experiments can be made to find a cure for Covid 19. There is reason to believe that no one to volunteer for the said facility, hence, the satirical essay. However, some realistic solutions and suggestions were mentioned.
