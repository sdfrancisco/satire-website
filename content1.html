Entry 1: Thoughts on Unemployement during COVID-19 (Opinionated)

My opinion on Unemployement and COVID-19 is that both should not be a problem in society, to the point that it affects even the hard working people. What i want to see in this country, for a start, is that the country implement ways for people to work, even in these times, by paralellizing work, thus speeding up processes and freeing time to do more recreational activities. A way to paralellize work is to give people work that they are assigned to do for the day/week/month, where it is up to them to do this. This will be done by multiple people, where work that is being relied on to be done by other employees will have to be done earlier. My second opinion is the employers and the government make job slots where people can work at home, like data processing, document evaluation and research, which does not rely too much on other people to be able to work. And last for this entry, I'd want the ability to be able to apply for jobs easily if i was looking for them. Something like an employee-employer matcher s ystem, where the employees can look for employers that they qualify for, and employers can recieve job requests and accept them based on their resume.

Entry 3: Thoughts on Unemployement (Realistic)

For real solutions, government and the private sector should work hand in hand and create an environment that would make jobs more available. The government should stir the economy by spending money on infrastructure and lending to the private sector. This will create jobs and address the unemployment. Invest on education so people are skilled and therefore can likely find a job. Make a survey of those unemployed, their skills and reason for not being unemployed. Match it up to the requirements of the private companies and help them get a job. Encourage people to open business by lending capital to them. Spread development in the rural areas to discourage migration to urban areas. This will also solve overcrowding in urban areas. Solve the peace and order situation of our country. This will encourage foreign investments and therefore create more jobs and job opportunities. Addressing the over population and environment degradation problem will also help in the unemployment issue. Lastly but most importantly , stop corruption. Need I say more?
